Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a K-Pop boy group that debuted in 2018 under JYP Entertainment, with the leader Bang Chan having hand-picked every member, which are Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, HAN, Felix, Seungmin and I.N. Having a subdivision of producers called '3RACHA' consisting of Bang Chan, Changbin, and HAN, Stray Kids have self-produced their way from humble beginnings to the very top of the industry, and became the first ever K-Pop group in history to achieve 7 consecutive wins on the Billboard 200 chart as of the year 2025.

They are the pioneers who have redefined the boundaries of K-Pop music and culture with their innovative concepts and unique "noise", standing by the belief that being different isn't being wrong. While their music has built a loyal fanbase called 'STAY', their loyalty is mostly built on their love for the tight-knit family bond between the members. The members of Stray Kids are each other's biggest supporters, as they share the same goal of achieving even greater heights and reciprocating their love for the STAYs all over the globe, just as their slogan goes:

'STRAY KIDS EVERYWHERE ALL AROUND THE WORLD. YOU MAKE STRAY KIDS STAY'
